Bio - Brian Omondi

Get to meet Brian Omondi

Who Am I?

Brian Omondi is an organized and result-driven electrical engineering technologist with over two years of experience in safety-focused projects across various industries, including agriculture, energy, healthcare, and transport. Known for his high resilience and innovative approach, Brian is dedicated to contributing decisively to his field, continuously seeking new skills and aiming to exploit cutting-edge electrical and electronics technology.

Brian's expertise lies in increasing the efficiency and reliability of electronics and autonomous systems for organizations. He possesses diverse skills in Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), Internet of Things (IoT), secured wireless communication, Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC), project design, and project management.

In addition to his technical skills, Brian is equipped with strong relational intelligence and excels in organizing, scheduling, and prioritizing both personal and team initiatives. He consistently strives to achieve a high standard in all his endeavours, even under pressure and tight deadlines.

Throughout his academic journey, Brian has held several notable responsibilities and accolades. He was recognized as the Best Student Coach in STEM in Murang'a County and the Most Innovative Student at Murang'a University of Technology. Brian also led research and development efforts at the university and was awarded Best Student in Agricultural Research and Innovation at the Science and Innovation Fair in Siaya County in both 2018 and 2019.

Brian's referees include influential figures such as the Project Manager of the Institute of Engineering Kenya (IEK-ESA MUT) at Murang'a University, the Founder of Smabril-Tech-Gen, the Chairperson of Future Innovators at Ambira High School, and the Sound Technologist at MUTCU. His notable project includes digitizing power monitoring devices.

Beyond his professional and academic achievements, Brian has a keen interest in team building, participating in social services, repairing broken electronics, attending developer hackathons, planting trees, and traveling to make new contacts. He is characterized by his strong organizational and time management skills, analytical and interpersonal abilities, attention to detail and accuracy, and the capacity to work both independently and as part of a team. Brian's proactive and innovative mindset, coupled with his problem-solving skills and resilience, makes him highly adaptable to changes in the environment and capable of working under minimal supervision
